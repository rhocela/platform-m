## Usage Guidelines

### Mermaid Diagrams
- **Primary state/node fill**: `#4EAEDC` (Blue) with white text
- **Primary border**: `#3581B8` (darker blue shade)
- **Role/actor fill**: `#E8F4F8` (light blue tint) with dark text
- **Success/active state fill**: `#6EC0A7` (Teal/Green) with white text
- **Success/active state border**: `#52A68F` (darker teal shade)

### Example Mermaid Styling
```mermaid
style NodeName fill:#4EAEDC,color:#FFFFFF,stroke:#3581B8
style ActiveNode fill:#6EC0A7,color:#FFFFFF,stroke:#52A68F
style RoleNode fill:#E8F4F8,color:#333333,stroke:#3581B8
```
